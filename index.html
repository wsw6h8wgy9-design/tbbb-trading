It started at seventeen with a few hundred dollars and the kind of obsession you don't learn — you bring it. A few thousand charts later, the screens are bigger and the size has changed, but the obsession is the same. The squeezes. The catalysts. The moment a tape finally tips.

TBBB exists because the most useful thing in trading isn't a signal — it's watching someone do it live and hearing why.

This room isn't a course, a guru promise, or a get-rich pitch. It's a screen and a microphone during market hours. You watch. You learn. You decide what to do with it.

No track record is shown publicly, no income claims are made, and nothing said in the room is a recommendation. The value is in the read and the reasoning — and the discipline of seeing one trader work the same setups, day after day, in real time.

Questions

The honest answers.

Is this financial advice?+
No. TBBB Trading Group is a live broadcast for educational and entertainment purposes only. Nothing in the room is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security. You are responsible for your own trading decisions.
Will you tell me what to buy?+
No. The room is built around watching one trader work — not telling you what to do. You see what's traded and hear the reasoning. What you do in your own account is entirely up to you.
What's traded?+
US equities — primarily short-squeeze setups, momentum names, news catalysts, and short setups when the tape calls for it. Day-trading focused, with occasional swing positions noted.
Do I need a big account to get value?+
No. The lessons are in the read and the reasoning, not the position size. Members watch and learn at every account level — the value is in seeing how a full-time trader frames decisions in real time.
What if I want to cancel?+
